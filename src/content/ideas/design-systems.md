How to build and maintain effective design systems that scale with your product.

## What is a Design System?

A design system is a collection of reusable components, guided by clear standards, that can be assembled together to build any number of applications.

## Benefits

- **Consistency**: Ensures a cohesive user experience across all products
- **Efficiency**: Reduces development time through reusable components
- **Scalability**: Makes it easier to maintain and update designs at scale

## Best Practices

1. Start with your most common components
2. Document everything thoroughly
3. Keep it simple and focused
4. Iterate based on real usage
